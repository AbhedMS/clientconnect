### 2. **PersonaPulse – Customer Behavior Modeling Suite**
PersonaPulse is a behavioral analytics toolkit that helps marketing and product teams decode customer intent. Using advanced clustering algorithms and sentiment analysis, it segments audiences based on purchasing behavior, engagement patterns, and psychographic traits. The suite includes tools for churn prediction, campaign optimization, and personalized recommendation engines. NeuraForge’s data scientists work closely with clients to fine-tune models for industry-specific needs. Ideal for e-commerce, telecom, and banking sectors, PersonaPulse empowers firms to craft hyper-targeted strategies that boost retention and lifetime value.
