### 5. **TalentScope – AI-Driven Workforce Analytics Service**
TalentScope is a strategic service offering that helps HR departments make smarter hiring, retention, and development decisions. NeuraForge’s team applies natural language processing to resumes, performance reviews, and internal communications to uncover hidden talent trends. Predictive models assess attrition risk, skill gaps, and leadership potential. The service includes a dashboard for workforce planning and scenario modeling. Whether you're scaling rapidly or navigating restructuring, TalentScope ensures your people strategy is data-informed and future-ready.
