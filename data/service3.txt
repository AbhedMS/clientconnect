**Evolver Finance – AI-Powered Transformation for the CFO Office**

**Overview**  
Evolver Finance radically transforms the core functions of the CFO’s office, enabling better strategic decision-making. It delivers real-time, trusted financial data to improve reporting quality, enhance financial risk insights, and optimize cost management. The platform is designed to help CFOs stay ahead in a complex business environment.

**Demo Experiences**

1. **Continuous Statutory Reporting**  
   ARIA continuously validates and assembles financial data, producing jurisdiction-ready reports near-instantly. This enables seamless compliance, audit-ready evidence, and allows CFOs to reallocate resources from manual reporting to strategic decision-making.

2. **Landed Cost Optimization**  
   ARIA recalculates landed costs in real time as tariffs, freight, and duties shift. It surfaces customs and trade intelligence, models scenarios, and highlights margin impacts instantly—giving CFOs actionable insights to optimize supply chains and protect profitability.
