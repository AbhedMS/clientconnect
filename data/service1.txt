**ARIA for Tax – Evolver’s AI-Powered Tax Intelligence**

**Overview**  
ARIA is Evolver’s AI-powered intelligence layer designed to transform the entire tax lifecycle. It scales with business needs, evolves with every review cycle, and turns tax into a strategic advantage. It supports tax teams from setup to close, enabling faster operations, compliance, and scalable growth.

---

**Key Benefits**
- **ROI on First Review Cycle**: Recover overpaid tax revenue and avoid audit penalties.
- **Reduction in Review Time**: ARIA significantly accelerates audit cycles without compromising quality.
- **Zero Compliance Issues**: Automatically analyzes millions of data rows to uncover deep insights.

---

**Core Capabilities**
- Reconciles tax filings to transactional data with full transparency.
- Uses advanced AI to analyze every transaction—not just samples.
- Automates:
  - Tax audit readiness reports
  - Tax look-back reviews
  - Tax research for new operations
- Integrates with SAP, Oracle, NetSuite, Ariba, and non-ERP systems.
- Minimizes financial statement impacts from overpaid/underpaid taxes.

**Performance Outcomes**
- **Speed**: Cuts review cycles from quarters to weeks using intelligent alerts.
- **Insights**: Provides tailored determinations that outperform generic rule sets.
- **Scale**: Handles millions of rows with complex tax logic.
- **Impact**: Enables real-time savings and peace of mind through ongoing use tax automation.

---

**ARIA Tax Modules**

1. **Tax Audit Readiness**  
   - Delivers audit readiness in weeks.
   - Analyzes 100% of transactional data across jurisdictions.
   - Processes both structured and unstructured documents (invoices, POs, exemption certificates).

2. **Tax Look Back Review**  
   - Automates comprehensive retrospective reviews.
   - Handles direct/indirect purchases and non-PO transactions.
   - Eliminates reliance on spreadsheets and sampling.

3. **Tax Classification Manager**  
   - Ensures accurate tax classification from product/service inception.
   - Supports SKU launches and legacy cleanup.
   - Embeds tax logic into the product lifecycle.

4. **Tax Research Assistant**  
   - Provides instant, legally reasoned answers.
   - Uses curated, version-controlled content (legislation, guidance, internal policy).
   - Trained specifically on jurisdictional tax rules with full legal traceability.

5. **Tax Cycle Manager**  
   - Automates monthly, quarterly, and annual tax closing.
   - Reviews large transactional reports, reconciles accounts, validates tax logic.
   - Surfaces anomalies and missed accruals without manual spreadsheets or checklists.

---

**Scalability**
ARIA supports end-to-end tax review across all tax types. It transforms how businesses manage classification, audits, reconciliations, and return preparation—built on explainable AI, not legacy logic.
